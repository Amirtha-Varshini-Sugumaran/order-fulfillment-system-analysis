# Stakeholder Analysis

| Role | Responsibility | Interest | Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| Operations Manager | Oversees delivery performance and daily workload | Wants clear visibility of active and delayed deliveries | High |
| Operations Coordinator | Creates orders and assigns drivers | Wants faster order entry and less manual follow-up | High |
| Customer Support Agent | Responds to customer delivery queries | Wants accurate status without calling operations | High |
| Driver | Completes assigned deliveries and provides updates | Wants clear delivery information and simple update steps | Medium |
| Senior Management | Reviews service performance and operational costs | Wants better reporting and fewer customer complaints | Medium |
| Sales Team | Manages customer relationships | Wants confidence that delivery promises can be checked | Medium |
| Finance Team | Uses completed delivery records for billing checks | Wants reliable completion records | Medium |
| IT Support | Supports system access and basic technical issues | Wants manageable permissions and low support overhead | Medium |
| Customers | Receive deliveries and request updates | Want clear and timely delivery information | High |
